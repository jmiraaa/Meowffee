YOU eat, WE donate!

We believe that every cat deserves a chance to feel loved, safe, and valued. That’s why our café isn’t just a place for coffee and cuddles it’s a heart-driven charity dedicated to rescuing stray and abandoned cats in need. We work tirelessly to provide medical care, food, shelter, and a safe space for these deserving kitties until they find their forever homes.
Every drink you enjoy and every visit you make helps support our mission. We partner with local animal shelters, rescue organizations, and veterinarians to ensure that each cat gets the care they deserve, whether it’s a simple check-up or life-saving surgery. Through our adoption program, we work to match each rescued cat with loving families, ensuring that they not only survive but thrive in a warm, caring environment.

Willing to donate?

Meowffee offers a love for felines with charity work by providing a safe haven for rescue cats, supporting adoptions, and raising funds for local animal welfare initiatives. Our café offers a cozy space where visitors can enjoy refreshments while helping cats find loving homes.
